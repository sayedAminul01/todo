Our Commitment to Privacy

At aW, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our productivity tools.

Information We Collect

Local Storage Only

aW Todo stores all your data locally in your browser's local storage. This means:

  • Your tasks and preferences never leave your device
  • We cannot access your personal todo items
  • Your data is completely under your control

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information including:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Task content or personal notes
  • Usage patterns or behavioral data
  • Location information

How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal data, we cannot use it for any purpose. All functionality of our Todo App operates entirely within your browser using local storage.

Third-Party Services

Our website may include advertisements served by Google AdSense. These services may collect information about your visits to this and other websites. For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

We may use cookies for:

  • Remembering your dark mode preference
  • Improving website functionality
  • Analytics (if implemented in the future)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over its security. We recommend:

  • Keeping your browser updated
  • Using secure devices
  • Regularly backing up important data

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
