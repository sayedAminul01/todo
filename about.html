Our Story

aW was founded in 2024 with a mission to create productivity tools that feel like they're from the future. Our team combines cutting-edge technology with intuitive design to deliver applications that enhance your workflow.

We understand that modern professionals need more than basic task lists. That's why aW Todo includes advanced features like due date tracking, smart filtering, data export/import, and comprehensive analytics - all while maintaining the simplicity you love.
